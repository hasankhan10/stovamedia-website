Building for hair transplant clinics required more than just static photos. Surgeons needed a way to design hairlines in 3D.

### Texture Mapping
Using Three.js r160, we implemented a custom shader to map 2D facial photos onto a generic 3D head model. This allowed patients to see their potential results in a realistic, 360-degree environment.

### Performance
By offloading vertex calculations to the GPU through custom GLSL, we kept the frame rate locked at 60fps, even on mid-range smartphones.
