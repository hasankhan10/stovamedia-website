Most clinic software in India is built for the **manager’s dashboard**, not the **patient’s reality**. In semi-urban India, network drops are common, 2G is frequent, and people hate downloading extra apps just to wait in a queue.

### The App Fatigue
Every new OPD system wants patients to download a 'Super App'. In Bharat, that's a non-starter. If it takes more than 30 seconds to join a queue, patients stick to paper.

### Our Solution
We built Mr Compounder to work over **WhatsApp and SMS**. No app, no account, just a digital token that updates in real-time.
